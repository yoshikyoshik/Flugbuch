3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers for our infrastructure:

  • Supabase: Database & Authentication (Server location: EU/Germany).
  • Netlify: Hosting of the web app and server functions.
  • Stripe: Payment processing. Credit card data is not stored on our servers but transmitted directly to Stripe.
  • API-Ninjas / FlightLabs: Retrieval of public airport data (no personal data).
